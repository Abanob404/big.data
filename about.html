Mission

At this time, we are witnessing a huge explosion in data. Analysis and processing of these data increases mainly in understanding and understanding customer requirements and thus increasing efficiency and productivity and reducing losses for companies. However, there are many challenges and constraints that hinder the use or expansion of large data and over time and technological advances, significant progress is expected in addressing the challenges and constraints of using large data more widely
